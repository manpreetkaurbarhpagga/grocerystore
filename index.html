About Our Store

Chaman Lal Karyana Store has been serving Village Parowal and surrounding areas since 1985. Founded by Shri Chaman Lal ji, our store has grown from a small shop to the village's most trusted grocery provider.

We take pride in offering authentic products at fair prices with personalized service that you won't find in big supermarkets. Our mission is to provide quality groceries with the warmth of traditional Indian hospitality.

Visit us today to experience the difference of shopping at a locally-owned store that cares about its customers.
